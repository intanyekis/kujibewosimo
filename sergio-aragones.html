    Biography

    Sergio Aragones is a popular person who is best known as a Cartoonist. Sergio was born on September 6, 1937, in Spain. By the 500th issue of Mad Magazine in 2009, Aragones was second to Al Jaffee in contributions to the magazine (424 issues to 451 issues). Sergio Aragones is a member of the famous Cartoonist list. Created the comic book Groo the Wanderer and provided work for Mad Magazine. An award, ‘The Sergio,’ was named after him and is given by the Comic Art Professional Society.

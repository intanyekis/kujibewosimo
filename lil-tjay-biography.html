    How many siblings does Lil Tjay have?

    Lil Tjay was born Tione Jayden Merritt on the 30th of April 2001 in South Bronx, New York. He is of All-American ancestry. His mother is from Ghana.

    He has two younger siblings.

    Early Life

    As a child, Lil Tjay was a troubled kid. When he was attending middle school, he was involved in fights with the boys in his neighborhood and school.

    He has also started doing a petty thief.

    However, he was a decent boy at home. When he would want to curse, he just moves out of home and curses outside.

    At the age of 15, he was in the youth detention center in New York for committing robbery. He spent a few of the months in the center.

    In the meantime, he realized that he has to change the path where he was. Finally, he decided to pursue music as his career.

    Lil Tjay- Professional Career

    – Lil Tjay started his career by uploading his singles like Resume and Brother on SoundCloud which turned out to be starting milestones of his career.

    Within the time interval of ten months, his tracks were played a million times. To date, he has accumulated 44.4 million plays.

    – In July 2018, he released None of Your Love which gathered 18.2 million plays in the short interval of eight months.

    The song featured the rappers like Lil Skies, Ty Dolla Sign, and YoungBoy Never Broke Again.

    – His tracks caught the eyes of the Columbia Recording Label. Under the recording label, he released his debut extended play, No Comparison. 

    – In January 2019, he featured the song, Pop Out along with Polo G.

    – On the 11th of October 2019, he released his debut studio album, True 2 Myself under Columbia recording Label. The album peaked at position #2 on the US Billboard.

    – Following the album, he released the album, F. N. Last on the 15th of October 2019, he released the official video of Hold On on his YouTube Channel.

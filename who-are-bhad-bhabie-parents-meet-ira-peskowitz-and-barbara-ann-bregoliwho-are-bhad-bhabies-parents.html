    Who is Bhad Bhabie?

    Danielle Bregoli, known as Bhad Bhabie, is a prominent American rapper, internet personality, and entrepreneur. Born on March 26, 2003, in Boynton Beach, Florida, she gained fame in 2017 with her viral catchphrase “Cash me outside, how bout dah?”

    Bhad Bhabie rose to prominence after appearing on an episode of Dr. Phil in September 2016. Her confrontational behavior and memorable catchphrase captured the internet’s attention, propelling her to instant meme sensation status. This led to numerous media appearances, including a segment on The Ellen DeGeneres Show.

    Capitalizing on her newfound fame, Bhad Bhabie entered the music industry and achieved success with her debut single, “These Heaux,” released in 2017. The song’s success made her the youngest female rapper to feature on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

    Continuing to release successful singles like “Gucci Flip Flops,” “I Got It,” and “Bestie,” her music is characterized by catchy melodies, relatable lyrics, and a confident attitude. She collaborated with various artists including Kodak Black, Lil Durk, and Asian Doll.

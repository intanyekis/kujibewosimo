    The Old Man Season 2 Renewed At FX

    Season 2 of The Old Man has been officially renewed for 2023

    The Old Man Season 2 Release Date

    There is no set date for a second season, but we hope the show will keep coming out once a year. If that happens, Season 2 would come out in the summer of 2023.

    The Old Man Season 2 Cast

    We still haven’t learned who will be removed as the season one finale draws near because it is still being released every week.

    Given that Dan Chase is the main character of the show, we can almost surely count on Jeff Bridges to reprise his role as him.

    The following actors should return in their roles, too:

    • Harold Harper, played by John Lithgow
    • Amy Brenneman portrays Zoe.
    • FBI agent Angela Adams is played by Alia Shawkat.
    • Raymond Waters played by EJ Bonilla
    • Julian Carson is portrayed by Gbenga Akinnagbe.

    Naturally, this depends on how well they perform in season 1.

    The Old Man Season 2 Plot

    Depending on how the finale plays out, one of our main concerns is if Chase will still be on the run. Or will anything else unite Chase and Harper instead of Angela’s safety? We currently only know that we can anticipate another crazy trip.

    We couldn’t be more excited to start the next part of this journey,” said executive producers Jonathan E. Steinberg, Warren Littlefield, and Dan Shotz in a statement following the series’ renewal for Season 2. The only thing we can think of to thank our friends at FX and 20th Television for their unrelenting patience, faith, and support in helping us finish Season 1 is to go deliver a Season 2 that sets the bar even higher.

    24 Jun 2024, 00:00

    - AmoMama in our diligent search for news has just realized from a post on Instagram that the American couple, Kirk and Tammy Franklin are very proud of their children.

    - Just recently, Tammy took to Instagram with picture posts of her three gorgeous children, and they have all grown up so much.

    - She first posted a throwback picture of her children, Carrington, Kennedy and Caziah, and then a more recent one and the changes were amazing.

    Kirk Franklin is a 48-year-old American gospel musician and author.

    He has also led some urban contemporary gospel choirs such as 'The Family,' 'God's Property' and 'One Nation Crew.'

    In many circles, Kirk Franklin has been dubbed the 'Reigning King of Urban Gospel,' and he has won quite some awards for his music including 12 Grammys and numerous Stellar Awards.

    Kirk officially tied the knot with his wife, Tammy, in 1996 with each having one child from a previous relationship, kirk's Kerrion and Tammy's Carrington.

    Together, they have two other children, Kennedy and Caziah who have grown up into beautiful young adults and their parents, especially Tammy are so proud of them.

    Just very recently, Tammy took to Instagram with pictures of her three children in an undeniable display of her love and her pride.

    In the posts which featured Carrington, Kennedy, and Caziah, she first posted a picture of them when they were much younger before she posted their more recent picture.

    If you think her children looked cute and adorable when they were young, wait till you see them all grown up.

    Although all three children bear a lot of resemblance to their mother, Carrington particularly looked like her mum; they could pass off as sisters if they tried.

    Tammy captioned the post with

    'My goodness, the time has flown! Carrington is now married, Kennedy turns 21 next week, and Caziah will soon be a senior in High School! They will forever be my babies!!! #swipeleft #helpmeholyghost #fbfriday'

    Fans had a lot to say about the post, and some of their comments are captured below.

    One fan, @paulinemikerudyauntie commented

    'Beautiful children they look just like you @iamtammyfranklin.'

    Another @mhowiemc wrote

    'Time waits for no one; they grow up so fast. Beautiful pictures. Blessed family'

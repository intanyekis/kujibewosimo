    The Equalizer 3 Begins Filming, First Look Photos Revealed

    Antoine Fuquas The Equalizer 3 began filming earlier this week in Boston, and though plot details are currently under wraps, we now have our first look at Denzel Washingtons Robert McCall on set. Check out the photos below!

    31 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Antoine Fuqua’s The Equalizer 3 began filming earlier this week in Boston, and though plot details are currently under wraps, we now have our first look at Denzel Washington’s Robert McCall on set. Check out the photos below!

    Plot details for The Equalizer 3 are currently being kept under wraps, but the sequel will see Denzel Washington’s Robert McCall coming out of retirement to once again serve justice to those who can’t do it for themselves. That mission will lead him into conflict with a mysterious man played by operators. Game of Thrones’ Pedro Pascal and Haley Bennett will also be back as Detective Brian Yankee and McCall’s only friend Miles Dyson, respectively.

    Antoine Fuqua returns to direct the sequel, which finds Denzel Washington reprising his role as vigilante Robert McCall. Joining him for the sequel are new cast members Dakota Fanning, Bill Pullman ( purge), and Melissa Leo ( Treme). In addition to Fuqua, Washington, and Richard Wenk return to pen the screenplay. The film is produced by Todd Black, Jason Blumenthal, and Steve Tisch of Escape Artists. Denis Leary, Mace Neufeld, Alex Siskin, and Tony Eldridge are executive producers. Sony Pictures has set the release date, for now, as 1st September 2023.

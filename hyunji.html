    Hyunji is a South Korean singer, musician, performer, dancer, and actor who has served as the lead vocalist of the South Korean girl group HashTag (stylized as haShtag or HASHTAG). Formed by Luk Factory, the group made its debut on October 11, 2017, with the release of the extended play The girl next door. HashTag‘s discography also includes other extended plays and tracks such as #Aeji #paSsion, My Style (어때보여), Freesm, Like That (그런거), When It Rains (비가 내리면), Hz, Love Game, and Hello This Night (안녕이밤). In addition to that, Hyunji was a member of the 5-member girl group Peach Girl which debuted on November 26, 2013, with its only single Fox-fur Muffler, before disbanding the following year.

    Hyunji Facts

  • From 2013 to 2014, she was a member of the girl group Peach Girl. Formed by JM Star Entertainment, the group’s other members were Mimi, Bogyeong, Somang, and Yuka.
  • Hyunji was cast as a nurse in the 2017 comedy film Daddy You, Daughter Me. Based on the 2006 Japanese novel Papa to Musume no Nanokakan by Takahisa Igarashi, the film stars Yoon Je-moon and Jung So-min.
  • Along with Ryu Hwa-young, Hyunji co-starred in the drama series Trace of the Hand in 2017.
  • On September 19, 2017, she was revealed as the 2nd member of the 5-member girl group HashTag.
  • Hyunji’s fellow HashTag members include Sua, Seungmin, Dajeong, Aeji, Subin, and Sojin. Dajeong and Aeji left the group in 2023.
  • HashTag released its debut extended play The girl next door on October 11, 2017, via Luk Factory and Genie Music and its tracklist includes songs like Hue (ㅇㅇ), Like That (그런거), and When It Rains (비가 내리면). The EP peaked at #41 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • On April 16, 2019, HashTag released its 2nd extended play titled #Aeji #paSsion and it peaked at #53 on the Gaon Album Chart. It consists of tracks such as Freesm, Hello This Night (안녕이밤), and Love Game.
  • Some of Hyunji’s talents include her impression of Donald Duck and her ability to sound like a cat.

    LOKI – the MCU series based on the charismatic Asgardian god of mischief – quickly won the hearts of the Marvel faithful following its premiere on Disney+ in June 2021. The show revolved around an alternate version of Loki, who created a new timeline and an alternate reality following the events of Avengers: Endgame.

    Marvel studio boss Kevin Feige confirmed that Loki is Marvel's most-watched Disney+ series, with over 5 billion minutes watched. Given its stellar cast ensemble, it is hardly surprising that the show garnered such a massive following.

    From recognizable A-listers such as Owen Wilson and the ever-endearing Tom Hiddleston to Sophia Di Martino, the cast dripped with talent and affluence. Let's dive into the deep pockets of the wealthiest cast members of the show.

    1 Sasha Lane As Hunter C-20: Net Worth $400,000

    Sasha Lane portrayed Hunter C-20 in the show, a TVA hunter kidnapped and later possessed by Sylvie to sabotage the TVA's plans. The Houston-born actress made her film debut in Andrea Arnold's award-winning American Honey, and has also appeared in films like Heart Beat Loud and The Miseducation Of Cameron Post.

    She has continued to prove her acting mettle with every role she has been offered thus far. Sasha holds a net worth of $400,000, and her acting chops and growing reputation will surely see her trajectory continue to soar both artistically and financially.

    2 Jonathan Majors As He Who Remains: Net Worth $2 Million

    In Loki season 1, the actor portrayed the variants of the MCU's latest big-bad, the all-powerful villain Kang the Conqueror. Majors, who made his acting debut in the ABC miniseries When We Rise in 2017, has already become a recognizable name in Hollywood.

    Despite being a new face in the acting scene, his impressive range and on screen charisma have earned him leading roles in blockbusters such as Creed III and The Harder They Fall. Jonathan Majors has accumulated a net worth of over $2 million, and paychecks from movies he featured in, like Creed III and Da 5 Bloods, which were commercially successful, earned him a significant amount.

    3 Richard E. Grant As Classic Loki: Net Worth $4 Million

    A classic actor for a classic character, this talented Swazi-born English actor has graced our screens for over four decades, and has featured in over 50 movies in that time. The veteran actor was cast as a classic, older version of our enigmatic god of mischief in the show's first season.

    Grant's extensive filmography has accumulated him a net worth of $4 million. With iconic roles in movies like Withnail & I and Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker, Grant's contribution to the industry has earned him both respect and frequent trips to the bank.

    4 Sophia Di Martino As Sylvie: Net Worth $5 million

    Sophie Di Martino has been a working actor since 2004 – but burst onto the scene with her role as Sylvie, a variant of Loki. Her portrayal of the mischievous variant won the hearts of fans and earned her "Best Breakthrough Performance" and "Best Team" (with Tom Hiddleston and Owen Wilson) at the 2022 MTV Movie and TV Awards.

    The English actress began her acting career on stage before transitioning to television and film, appearing in British TV shows such as Flowers and Mount Pleasant. Di Martino's net worth is currently estimated at $5 million, a figure poised to increase as she continues as a regular in the show's second season.

    5 Gugu Mbatha-Raw As Ravonna Renslayer: Net Worth $5 Million

    The British actress has amassed a fortune of over $5 million in the course of career to date. After completing her studies at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London, she embarked on her acting career in 2004, securing a role in the medical drama Holby City.

    Gugu Mbatha Raw also featured in highly-rated shows such as Doctor Who, Easy and lent her voice to a character in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance. She made her breakout role in the British drama Belle as the titular character, where she portrayed the daughter of a White naval officer and an enslaved woman set in the 18th century.

    Mbatha-Raw has consistently impressed audiences with her talent across many films and television genres. In the Loki series, her portrayal as the Time Variant Authority Judge Ravonna Renslayer served to reacquaint fans with her onscreen charisma.

    6 Tara Strong As Miss Minutes: Net Worth $10 million

    Tara Lyn Strong kicked off her acting career in a voice acting role in Hello Kitty's Furry Tale Theater. She has been present in the entertainment industry for over three decades.

    Strong has provided the voices of many popular characters in movies, such as Bubbles in The Powerpuff Girls, Raven in Teen Titans and Batgirl in The New Batman Adventures. The Canadian-American actress voiced the character of Miss Minutes, the animated anthropomorphic clock mascot of the TVA.

    Her net worth is estimated to be $10 million – owing to her successful career as a voice actor with an impressive filmography.

    7 Tom Hiddleston As Loki: Net Worth $40 million

    Tom Hiddleston embodied the role of the famous Asgardian god of mischief and made it his own. Besides his god tier acting skills, the British actor is known for his fat wallet.

    Hiddleston began his professional acting career in the theater before switching to television shows and movies, featuring in films like Unrelated, Archipelago and Midnight in Paris. The Golden Globe Award has been part of projects collectively, earning over half a billion dollars at the box office.

    He made his MCU debut as Loki in 2011's Thor – after initially auditioning for the role of the main character, Thor. Since then, Hiddleston has appeared in seven different MCU movies and starred as the titular character in Loki.

    Hiddleston has also signed numerous endorsement deals, with top brands like Jaguar and Gucci. This, coupled with his Hollywood A-list status, has seen his net worth rise to $40 million.

    8 Owen Wilson As Mobius M. Mobius: Net Worth $70 million

    Famous for his quick wit, distinctive voice, and signature charm, Owen Wilson needs no introduction. The man behind the analytical yet charming TVA agent Mobius M. Mobius is the highest earner in the entire Loki cast.

    The actor has undoubtedly made his mark on the entertainment industry with a career spanning almost three decades. Wilson made his film debut at age 27 in Wes Anderson's Bottle Rocket, and has since starred in blockbusters like Armageddon, Wedding Crashers and Meet the Fockers.

    The actor is estimated to be worth around $70 million, and his success can also be attributed to his lucrative endeavors in screenwriting and involvement in production. Whether he is crashing weddings or rearranging timelines alongside Loki himself, one thing is sure: Wilson lends his spark wherever he goes.

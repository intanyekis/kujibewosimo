    Dana Brooke has issued a statement following her WWE release.

    WWE released Dana Brooke in a wave of cuts on September 21. The former WWE 24/7 Champion took to Twitter on September 22 and commented on the news. She made it clear that WWE has been her fairytale, and she entered a new world when she started the journey. Brooke stated that she gave her everything to the business.

    She also wrote this is not the end of her fairytale. Instead, she stated that she will be moving forward to the next chapter. Brooke noted that this is a rocky chapter, but she will keep going. She emphasized that the fairytale will continue.

    Brooke signed with WWE in 2013 and moved to the main roster in 2016. She had a few stints on Raw, SmackDown, and she also returned to NXT in 2023. During her time with WWE, she held the 24/7 Championship 15 times.

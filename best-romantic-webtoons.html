    If you love getting lost in a toe-curlingly sweet romance novel or watching a rom-com that gives you butterflies, then romantic webtoons are going to be your next big obsession.

    For those not in the know, webtoons are another South Korean export that is taking over the world one comic strip at a time. With complex and well-written storylines, stunning illustrations and intricate world-building, these digital comics are addictive and encompass a wide range of genres, so there’s something for everyone.

    Many of our favourite K-dramas are also based on webtoons, like Business Proposal, Itaewon Class or True Beauty.

    Like K-dramas, webtoons have also mastered the art of serving up romance stories that will stay with you for a long time. The popularity of the medium has grown so much that artists from other countries like US and Japan have also started creating webtoons. From hilarious romantic comedies to sweeping historical sagas, these are the romantic webtoons that you must read right away…

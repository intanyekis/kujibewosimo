    Nick Lachey and Vanessa Lachey really are quite the pair. Not are not only the hosts of Love Is Blind, but they are also a happily married couple in real life! The pair even share a Nov. 9 birthday! How weird is that?

    Nick and Vanessa met when she starred in his music video for “What’s Left Of Me” in 2006, just after he and Jessica Simpson divorced. Despite a brief split in 2009, the couple got engaged in 2010 and married the following year. Soon after, they began expanding their family and had three kids: Camden John Lachey, Phoenix Robert Lachey, and Brooklyn Elisabeth Lachey.

    While they didn’t meet on their dating show, Vanessa has compared their relationship to how the contestants date through a wall and have to get to know each other emotionally. Since they dated long distance, “it was a lot of texting or phone calls — laying in bed at night, getting to know each other,” she said on the HollywoodLife podcast. “Then, when we saw each other, everything was elevated and escalated. It was even that much more exciting, because we had this emotional relationship.”

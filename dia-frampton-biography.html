    Who is Dia Frampton?

    Dia Frampton is a popular American singer, songwriter, and actress.

    She is well known as the lead singer of the Meg & Dia band and also as the runner-up in the very first season of The Voice.

    Frampton also occasionally acts in movies and TV shows.

    Most recently, she played the main role in the 2020’s I Hate New Year’s.

    Dia Frampton: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

    Dia Frampton was born on the 2nd of October, 1987 in Draper, Utah, U.S.A.

    As of now, her age is 35. She belongs to the American nationality and her ethnicity is mixed.

    Dia Frampton was born to her father Dix Frampton and his mother Suhee Frampton. Her father comes from English and Dutch ancestry whereas her mother is originally from South Korea.

    Dia grew up in the U.S. with a passion for music. She shared this passion with her sister Meg Frampton and the two would later go on to form the successful band, Meg & Dia.

    Education

    Talking about her education, Dia attended both Dixie High School and Shadow Ridge High School during her school years.

    Dia did not attend college but rather chose to pursue a career in music after high school. So, she graduated high school early and soon moved to Salt Lake City with her sister to build a music career.

    Dia Frampton: Professional Life, Career

    Dia started her music career by forming the Meg and Dia band along with her sister.

    The band released its debut album titled Our Home is Gone in 2005.

    The sisters used social media, more specifically MySpace to their advantage and promoted their music on the platform.

    This way, they were able to reach a larger audience and also gained a lot of fans.

    As a result of all this, their subsequent album, Something Real peaked at no 12 on the Billboard Heatseekers Chart.

    The band also performed as the official MySpace band on the Warped Tour 2006.

    Later in 2011, Dia Frampton competed in the first season of the Voice as a contestant and ended up as the runner-up at the end of the season.

    Unfortunately, the band split up soon after and Meg and Dia went their separate ways. Dia was quite successful in her solo music career as well.

    Her first solo album Red peaked at no 1 on the Billboard Heatseekers Chart in the U.S, and her 2nd album Bruises also performed well.

    Dia also worked on a few EPs and singles and worked together with other artists on their songs as well.

    Dia also pursued acting, though not too seriously, with minor roles and cameos in movies and TV shows such as The Crooked Man and Criminal Minds.

    More recently Dia Frampton landed the main role in 2020’s I Hate New Year’s. Meg and Dia also reunited recently in 2019. Since then, the band has released an album, a Christmas single, and even went on tour to support their album.

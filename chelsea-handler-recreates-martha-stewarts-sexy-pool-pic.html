    Chelsea wants what Martha has.

    On Tuesday, Martha Stewart donned a bathing suit and posted a sultry swimming pool selfie that social media fell head-over-heels for. Comedian Chelsea Handler took to Instagram Wednesday to re-create the 78-year-old lifestyle guru’s thirst trap.

    “I’m here to take @marthastewart48’s swimming lesson. If I look this good at 78 I better still have a pool to show myself off in,” Handler, 45, wrote on Instagram.

    Fans were lapping up the copycat post.

    “Omg so freaking good,” wrote Nicole Scherzinger, while Katie Couric reacted with a simple “😊” emoji.

    However, it seems Stewart wasn’t so delighted. “I’m so happy that you liked my post well enough to emulate it,” she critiqued. “I do think my pool is a little bit prettier than yours and that my facial expression is a little more relaxed.”

    “Nevertheless you are a beautiful girl and F--KING too young to be emulating me,” she said, changing her tune. “Continue with your comedy, your humor, and your amazing ability to gather around you political liberals. We need it! Love Martha.”

    This isn’t the only selfie of Handler’s to make waves. Recently, she posed wearing protective face masks as a bra. She’s also used books to cover up her naked body.

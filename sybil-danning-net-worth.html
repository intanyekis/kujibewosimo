    Many people know Sybil Danning as the Austrian sex symbol who made her way into Hollywood in the ’80s. Danning at the peak of her career was one of the hottest celebrities on the planet.

    One of the first actresses to combine absolute beauty with a stunning physique, Danning won the hearts of many. Unfortunately, her beautiful body changed with her age and the 67-year-old actress isn’t the same on the outside anymore but she has earned a massive amount of money from her career. Let’s find out what she earned and her net worth.

    The Queen of B Movies is a Muti-Millionaire

    Sybil Danning is not just an ordinary actor. She appeared in more than 75 movies throughout her career among which almost all are B movies. From her career in movies she garnered more money than most B movie actors working today.

    Danning has an estimated net worth of $3 million. Most of her net worth comes from her appearance in movies during the ’80s and ’90s. Most of her notable role includes her role as Stirba in Howling II: Your Sister Is a Werewolf where she appeared alongside famous actor Christopher Lee.

    Danning’ Investments Yield a Great Return

    The former Playboy model is not just an actress but she also has a good eye for great investments. Along with her partner, she owned shares in SC Riessersee, a German ice hockey team; however, she sold all her shares in the year 2003.

    In 2011, Danning made headlines by saying that they were about to buy an English football team, Sheffield Wednesday. But later the deal did not happen between them and then-owner Milan Mandaric. Mandaric later said that he never met or even talked to Danning and also that he would sell the team only to someone who can take it forward.

    The Hercules hitmaker was a model way before her acting career started. She appeared on covers of many magazines including Playboy. From her modeling career, she earned an estimated amount of $300,000 which is worth way more today.

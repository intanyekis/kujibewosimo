    Drew Barrymore and Shae Hong To Release 'Cook With Beautiful' Kitchenware Line

    As if Drew Barrymore hasn't done enough to make her fans love her, she's decided to team up with Made By Gather CEO, Shae Hong, to create a line of trendy green kitchenware appliances that are sure to sell out as fast as her favorite new air fryer did.

    11 Jul 2024, 00:00

    As if Drew Barrymore hasn't done enough to make her fans love her, she's decided to team up with Made By Gather CEO, Shae Hong, to create a line of trendy green kitchenware appliances that are sure to sell out as fast as her favorite new air fryer did.

    In less than twenty-four hours, her air fryer offering from her new Cook With Beautiful line sold out at Walmart stores across the country, making a fantastic showing for all the new items that her line will have for new and old fans alike.

    The line so far includes the air fryer, a programmable 14 cup coffee maker, toaster, a one touch kettle, and a blender, all in the same calming, subdued shade of green.

    Related: Why Drew Barrymore Is Inspired By Howard Stern

    Of course, Barrymore is no stranger to the business world. She is also the imaginative force behind Flower Beauty cosmetics and Flower Home, which is another of her exclusive Walmart offerings. It seems that her kitchen appliance line is sure to do just as well, and may even outshine her other efforts.

    Either way, Barrymore is sure to be pleased with how well her new venture is doing, and on her Instagram feed, she credits Hong for his friendship and beautiful partnership.

    Barrymore has recently said that she's taking a step out of the limelight for now - between her new talk show and her two children, she feels that she no longer has time for acting the way she used to. It's clear, however, that Clarkson has no shortage of other passions to keep herself busy with.

    It's safe to say that if this venture turns out like most of the things in Barrymore's life, it'll turn up roses for the former actress, who is already a successful mother to Olive and Frankie.

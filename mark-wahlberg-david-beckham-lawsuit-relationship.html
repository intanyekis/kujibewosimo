    From the outside looking in, it has long seemed like Mark Wahlberg and David Beckham both have idyllic lives. After all, Wahlberg is incredibly rich and successful since he has starred in many blockbuster movies and Beckham is very wealthy thanks to being a superstar athlete.

    Unfortunately for Wahlberg, however, Beckham has become a pain since he notably sued the actor in 2024. Years before that, Wahlberg also publicly revealed how annoyed he was with Beckham when he complained about the athlete and his Spice Girls wife Victoria Beckham.

    This article will reveal why Mark Wahlberg once complained about David and Victoria Beckham. Then, the article will look at why David Beckham sued Wahlberg in 2024.

    Mark Wahlberg Was Mad That David Beckham And Victoria Beckham Moved Near His Family

    At one point in Mark Wahlberg's life, the Hollywood actor and former rapper seemingly loved living a hard lifestyle. After all, as fans of Entourage will already know, the show which included a lot of partying was partly based on Wahlberg's real life.

    However, after becoming a husband and father, Wahlberg seems to like a quieter lifestyle. As a result, when David and Victoria Beckham began living near him, Wahlberg complained about how they ruined the neighborhood during a 2009 Daily Mail interview.

    "Man, the Beckhams. It used to be so quiet on my road. Then David moves in with his family. Suddenly we’ve got paparazzi hanging out day and night. Now they’ll follow any car that drives down the road. I take strong exception to that kind of thing. When I take my kids to the park I don’t want strangers sticking their lenses into our faces."

    At the time of Wahlberg's remarks, David and Victoria Beckham had been living in America for a short while. The superstar athlete and his family made the massive move after the shocking news that he joined the LA Galaxy.
